Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SESMine platform and services

How do I get started with SESMine?
+
Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, choose your subscription plan, and access our comprehensive onboarding tutorials. Our support team is available to guide you through the initial setup and help you configure the platform for your specific mining operations.
What types of mining operations does SESMine support?
+
SESMine supports all major mining operations including open-pit mining, underground mining, mineral processing, and quarrying. Our platform is designed to handle various commodities such as gold, copper, iron ore, coal, and industrial minerals with specialized tools for each operation type.
Is my data secure on the SESMine platform?
+
Yes, security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, regular security audits, and comply with international data protection standards. Your mining data is protected with multi-layer security protocols and access controls.
Can I integrate SESMine with existing systems?
+
Absolutely! SESMine offers comprehensive API integration capabilities and supports connections with popular mining software, ERP systems, and IoT devices. Our integration team can help you connect your existing systems seamlessly.
What training and support do you provide?
+
We offer comprehensive training programs including online courses, live webinars, on-site training, and certification programs. Our support includes 24/7 technical assistance, dedicated account managers, and extensive documentation and video tutorials.
What are the pricing options?
+
We offer flexible pricing plans including Starter, Professional, and Enterprise tiers. Pricing is based on the number of users, features required, and data storage needs. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.
