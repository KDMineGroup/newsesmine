Empowering the global mining industry with intelligent solutions and expert knowledge

Our Story

SESMine was founded with a vision to revolutionize the mining industry through technology and innovation. We recognized that mining professionals needed better tools to make informed decisions, optimize operations, and manage complex projects efficiently.

Born from decades of combined mining expertise and a passion for technological advancement, SESMine emerged as a comprehensive platform that bridges the gap between traditional mining practices and modern digital solutions.

Today, we serve over 1,200 mining professionals worldwide, providing them with industry-leading cost estimation tools, financial modeling capabilities, and real-time analytics. Our platform is built on the foundation of AACE International standards and continuously evolves with the industry's needs.
