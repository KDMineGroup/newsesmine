FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does SESMine offer?
SESMine offers comprehensive mining solutions including Engineering Services, Economics & Financial Analysis, Consulting, Procurement & Supply Chain Management, Innovation & Technology Solutions, Analytics Platform, and Training & Education programs.
How can I request a quote for your services?
You can request a quote by filling out the contact form on this page, emailing sales@sesmine.com, or calling our sales team directly. We'll respond within 24 hours with a customized proposal.
Do you offer training programs for mining professionals?
Yes! We offer over 250 professional training courses covering all aspects of mining operations, from basic fundamentals to advanced specialized topics. Visit our Training & Education Hub for more information.
What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Small consulting projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive engineering studies can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! We have offices in 6 continents and serve clients in over 85 countries. Our team is experienced in working across different time zones, languages, and regulatory environments.
How do I access the Analytics Platform?
You can sign up for a free trial of our Analytics Platform through our website. After registration, you'll receive login credentials and access to our comprehensive dashboard and tools.
