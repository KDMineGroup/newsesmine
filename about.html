Our Story

SESMine was founded with a vision to revolutionize the mining industry through technology and innovation. We recognized that mining professionals needed better tools to make informed decisions, optimize operations, and manage complex projects efficiently.

Today, we serve over 1,200 mining professionals worldwide, providing them with industry-leading cost estimation tools, financial modeling capabilities, and real-time analytics. Our platform is built on decades of combined mining expertise and cutting-edge technology.
