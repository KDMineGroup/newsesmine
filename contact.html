FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your response time for support inquiries?
+
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support, our 24/7 live chat provides immediate assistance. Premium support customers receive priority response within 2 hours.
Can I schedule a product demo?
+
Yes! We offer personalized product demonstrations tailored to your specific needs. You can schedule a demo through our contact form, by calling us directly, or using the "Schedule Meeting" option above. Demos typically last 30-60 minutes.
Do you offer on-site consulting services?
+
Yes, we provide on-site consulting services globally. Our team of expert consultants can visit your operation to conduct assessments, provide training, and implement solutions. Contact our consulting team for availability and pricing.
What languages do you support?
+
Our platform and support are available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, and Mandarin. Additional language support can be arranged for enterprise clients. Our global team ensures you receive assistance in your preferred language.
How can I become a partner?
+
We're always interested in strategic partnerships. Please fill out the contact form selecting "Partnership" as the inquiry type, or email partnerships@sesmine.com directly. Our business development team will review your proposal and respond within 5 business days.
