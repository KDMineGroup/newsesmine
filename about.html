About SESMine

Transforming mining operations through innovative technology and expert solutions since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, SESMine emerged from a vision to revolutionize the mining industry through cutting-edge technology and data-driven solutions. Our founders, seasoned mining engineers and technology experts, recognized the critical need for accurate, efficient, and AACE-compliant cost estimation tools in the mining sector.

Today, SESMine serves over 1,200 mining professionals across 45 countries, providing comprehensive solutions for cost estimation, financial modeling, and project analytics. Our platform has been instrumental in optimizing over $2.5 billion in mining project costs, maintaining an industry-leading 98% accuracy rate.

We combine deep mining industry expertise with advanced AI and machine learning technologies to deliver solutions that empower mining companies to make informed decisions, reduce costs, and improve operational efficiency.

Our Mission

To empower mining professionals worldwide with intelligent, accurate, and efficient tools that transform how mining projects are planned, estimated, and executed. We strive to set new standards in mining cost estimation and project analytics.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for mining intelligence platforms, driving innovation and sustainability in the mining industry through technology, data, and expert knowledge.

Our Core Values

Accuracy

We maintain the highest standards of precision in all our cost estimations and analytics, ensuring reliable data for critical decisions.

Innovation

Continuously pushing boundaries with AI and machine learning to deliver cutting-edge solutions for the mining industry.

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships with clients, experts, and industry leaders to create comprehensive solutions.

Sustainability

Committed to supporting sustainable mining practices through efficient resource planning and optimization.
