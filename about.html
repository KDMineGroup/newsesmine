Leading the digital transformation of the global mining industry through innovative engineering solutions, advanced analytics, and cutting-edge technology.

Our Story

Building the Future of Mining

Founded in 2015 by a team of mining engineers and technology innovators, SES_MINE was born from a vision to revolutionize the mining industry through digital transformation. We recognized that traditional mining operations were facing unprecedented challenges: increasing costs, declining ore grades, environmental pressures, and safety concerns.

Our founders, with over 100 years of combined experience in mining engineering, project management, and software development, set out to create a comprehensive platform that would address these challenges head-on. Starting with a small team of 12 passionate individuals, we developed our first cost estimation tool that quickly gained traction in the industry.

Today, SES_MINE has grown into a global leader in mining technology solutions, serving over 500 companies across 75 countries. Our platform has been used to plan and optimize projects worth over $50 billion, helping our clients save billions in costs while improving safety and sustainability.

We continue to innovate, investing heavily in AI, automation, and emerging technologies to ensure our clients stay ahead in an increasingly competitive and complex industry. Our commitment remains unchanged: to empower mining companies with the tools and insights they need to build a more efficient, safe, and sustainable future.

Our Mission

To empower mining companies worldwide with intelligent, data-driven solutions that optimize operations, reduce costs, enhance safety, and promote sustainable practices. We strive to be the trusted technology partner that transforms traditional mining into smart, efficient, and responsible operations.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading platform for mining intelligence and innovation, setting the standard for digital transformation in the industry. We envision a future where every mining operation leverages advanced technology to achieve optimal performance while minimizing environmental impact and maximizing value for all stakeholders.
