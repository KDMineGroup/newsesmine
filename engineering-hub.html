About Engineering Hub

The Engineering Hub provides comprehensive tools and resources for mining engineering professionals. Our AACE-compliant cost estimation tools ensure accuracy and reliability in project planning and budgeting. Access our extensive equipment database, create detailed work breakdown structures, and collaborate with your team in real-time.


All tools are regularly updated with the latest industry standards and best practices. Our technical library contains thousands of documents, case studies, and guidelines to support your engineering decisions.
